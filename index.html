Welcome to CEFI Uganda

Year Founded: 2012

CEFI (Child Education Focus Initiative) was founded in 2012 by a group of individuals from diverse professional backgrounds who shared a passion for improving children's wellbeing. Our organization is dedicated to protecting and advocating for children's rights, promoting education, and fostering community development.

Our Mission

CEFI is a Christian, child protection, development, and advocacy organization dedicated to working with children, families, and communities to overcome poverty and other injustices. Our mission is to ensure a brighter future for vulnerable children through education, protection, and comprehensive community support.

Vision

To create a world where every child, youth, orphan, and vulnerable child, regardless of background or circumstance, has access to the tools, support, and opportunities necessary for educational success and personal growth.

Core Values

  • Inclusivity: We believe in equal educational opportunities for all children.
  • Empowerment: We strive to empower children, educators, and communities.
  • Collaboration: We work in partnership with families, schools, and community organizations.
  • Innovation: We are committed to adopting and promoting innovative educational practices.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our actions.

Key Objectives

  • Support: Provide educational resources and support services to children and families in need.
  • Advocacy: Advocate for policies and practices that promote equitable education.
  • Collaboration: Partner with schools, communities, and other organizations to enhance educational opportunities.
  • Innovation: Develop and implement innovative educational programs and strategies.
  • Awareness: Raise awareness about the importance of quality education for all children.

Achievements

Since its inception in 2012, CEFI has:

  • Established two primary schools: Brilliantkids Kindergarten and Junior School, Riana Junior School, and a vocational institution called CEFI Comprehensive Institute.
  • Reached over 70,000 children through its various programs and services.
  • Distributed thousands of educational resources to schools and communities in need.
  • Partnered with over 50 educational institutions and community organizations.
  • Successfully advocated for policy changes that have improved access to quality education in several districts.
