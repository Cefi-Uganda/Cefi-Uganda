Welcome to CEFI Uganda

Year Founded: 2012

CEFI (Child Education Focus Initiative) was founded in 2012 by a group of individuals from diverse professional backgrounds who shared a passion for improving children's wellbeing. Our organization is dedicated to protecting and advocating for children's rights, promoting education, and fostering community development.

Our Mission

CEFI is a Christian, child protection, development, and advocacy organization dedicated to working with children, families, and communities to overcome poverty and other injustices. Our mission is to ensure a brighter future for vulnerable children through education, protection, and comprehensive community support.

Our Objectives

  • Education: Providing access to quality education for all children, ensuring they have the tools and resources necessary for their academic and personal development.
  • Child Protection: Advocating for and implementing measures to protect children from abuse, neglect, and exploitation.
  • Community Development: Empowering communities through sustainable development projects that improve living conditions and promote economic growth.

Registration

CEFI Uganda is a registered Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) operating in the Wakiso District of Uganda. We are recognized under charity number 12446 and registration number S.5914/12620.
